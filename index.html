VINNY ✵ SHAW

Photography

Having spent the best part of 20 years working and touring as a Concert Lighting Designer, playing with shape, colour and movement, I began developing a strong urge to capture moments of light, as well as create them. While I greatly enjoy travelling and experiencing other cities and countries, a vast of my career has been spent in busy, crowded environments. As a result, the need to explore the calmer, peaceful places of nature has grown for me. In a strange twist of fate, just before the Covid Pandemic hit, I relocated back to the Highlands of Scotland, where I grew up. This has given me easy access to a vast playground of wilderness and scenery right on my doorstep, where I can continue to capture the compositions that catch my eye.
